 Contextual menus
    There are a couple of useful contextual menus available in iTerm. The first one is availabel by right or control clicking in the text view of a tab. This brings up options to launch new sessions or select among the sessions already open. When the command key is held down while bringing up the contextual menu, new sessions are opened in new windows. Also, selected URLs can also be sent to the default web browser and email client. You can also command-click on URLs to launch them. Finally, other standard functions such as copy, paste, save, and close are also available.
    The other contextual menu is availble by right or control clicking on a session's tab widget. This menu provides options to select among open tabs and to move a specific tab to a separate window.
